# Navigating AAAI: Where to Submit for the Best Odds

After diving deep into AAAI's track-level acceptance rates, I discovered some surprising patterns that could help researchers maximize their chances...

## Key Findings

### High-Acceptance Tracks

| Track | Est. Acceptance | Why It Works |
|-------|--------------|--------------|
| Emerging Trends | ~35-40% | Designed for speculative work |
| AI Alignment | ~30-35% | Newer, fewer submissions |
| AI for Social Impact | ~30-35% | Applied focus |

### The Saturated Zones

- **LLM papers**: ~15-18% (everyone submits here)
- **Computer Vision**: ~15-18% (wrong venue)
- **Deep Learning Theory**: ~15-18% (go to NeurIPS)

## My Strategy

I'm targeting the **Emerging Trends** track with a novel neuro-symbolic approach. The key insight? AAAI rewards intellectual courage over compute budgets.
